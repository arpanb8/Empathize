What is Empathize?


Empathize is an artificially intelligent chatbot that is emotionally aware and can Empathize with people. It will provide an outlet for people to vent out their everyday problems and communicate with our compassionate bot. Our founder Arpan has personally reached out to dozens of troubled teens - most of whom he met on LGBTQ groups on Facebook - and stopped them from doing irreversible harm. With a huge success rate and a ton of motivation from his friends, he is humbled by his experiences. In the short term, our goal is to offer a platform for everybody to let out how they feel and get emotional support. In the long term, however, we seek to improve the way mental health problems are perceived and approached in our society.
